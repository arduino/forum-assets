Discussion about the **Nesso N1**

The **Arduino Nesso N1** is an all-in-one development board, with integrated touchscreen display, Wi-Fi, and LoRa radio.

Use this category for topics where the primary subject matter is the **Arduino Nesso N1**.

The **Nesso N1** is powered by the **ESP32-C6** microcontroller. The hardware comes ready to use in enclosure. It offers the following integrated features:

- Color touchscreen LCD display
- LiPo battery
- Wi-Fi and Lora radios
- Power programmable buttons
- IMU
- Buzzer
- IR transmitter
- Grove, M5StickC HAT, and Qwiic connectors
